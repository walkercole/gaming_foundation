Dark Souls is the first installment of the Dark Souls: Trilogy and is widely recognized as the start to one of the best game series made to date.

Made by studio FromSoftware, Dark Souls is a dark themed third-person RPG that delivers unique and challenging gameplay driven by an equally unique passionate fan-base.
